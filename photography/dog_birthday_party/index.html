Dog Birthday Party

This was a fun one. I had never been to a party for a dog before, but there's a first time for everything. I brought along my camera in an attempt to practice shooting bright subjects. That specific aim came from the fact that the star of the show - the wonderful Ms. Genevieve - was a samoyed. Accompanying her were the guests - Lilli and Charlie. All were extremely polite and very photogenic.

That being said, this was a difficult shoot and far from my best work. There are gems in here for sure, but many of the shots were conducted either while the dogs were moving, in low-light conditions, or both. To put it frankly, I was in over my head with this one. Still, I learned a lot about shooting live subjects and had a great time in the process.
