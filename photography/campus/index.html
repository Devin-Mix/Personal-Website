The University of Florida Campus

After the Kanapaha shoot, I had a few shots left in the roll I'd been working with. With my graduation from the University of Florida on the horizon, I thought it would be a good idea to take a few shots of the place I'd spent four years of my life.

I'd read on the UFL subreddit that there was a tree blooming near the Reitz North Lawn, so that became the focus of my shoot. Doing just that felt dull though, so I threw a few other things in there as well.
