Kanapaha Botanical Gardens

The Kanapaha Botanical Gardens are located just West of Gainesville, FL and contain a variety of species. For a small fee, visitors are welcome to walk the gardens and connect with nature. I strongly recommend visiting.

These shots were easily some of my best at the time of their creation. Armed with a tripod, good lighting, and a desire to learn, I embarked through Kanapaha and did my best to capture what they had to offer.
