Car Meet

Not long after shooting my first roll, I was invited by my good friend Matthew to attend a car meet with him and his Ford Focus ST. Matthew is a long-term car enthusiast and member of the Gainesville, FL car scene. While his dedication to that community and automotive enthusiasm far exceeds my own, cars were one of several mutual interests on which our friendship was initially built. As such, it was fitting for this event to be among the last we were able to enjoy together in college.

Regarding the shoot itself, this was a much different set of circumstances than that I had dealt with previously. For one, I had procured a tripod to deal with the longer exposure times I expected to deal with. Still, the lighting was obviously low, so I did my best to take advantage of it rather than be hindered by it.
